About Ready Reckoner Rates

The Ready Reckoner Rate (RR Rate) is the minimum value set by the Maharashtra government for property transactions in different areas. These rates are revised annually and serve as the basis for calculating stamp duty and registration charges.

Our portal provides the official 2025 Ready Reckoner rates for Nashik city and surrounding villages, helping property buyers, sellers, and professionals estimate property values and associated costs.
